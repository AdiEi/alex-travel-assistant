Alex:  Hi! I'm Alex, your personal travel assistant.
       Where are you headed — or not sure yet?

User:  What should I pack?

Alex:  I'd love to help! First, what's your destination?

User:  Thailand

Alex:  Great choice! When are you planning to travel?

User:  I think about March

Alex:  Perfect! How many days are you staying?

User:  14 days

Alex:  Fantastic! What activities are you planning?
       (beach, hiking, city exploring, formal dining)

User:  A mix of hiking and beaches

Alex:  14-day Thailand packing list for March — hiking + beaches:

       Clothing:
       - Lightweight quick-dry hiking pants and shorts
       - Breathable t-shirts and tank tops
       - Swimsuits (2-3)
       - Light jacket for cooler evenings
       - Flip-flops + sturdy hiking shoes

       Accessories:
       - Sun hat, sunglasses, lightweight daypack
       - Beach towel, reef-safe sunscreen
       - Insect repellent

       Essentials:
       - Reusable water bottle
       - Camera, travel adapter

       [Photo of Thailand displayed in chat]

User:  Thanks! Bye bye!

Alex:  Happy travels! 🌍 I hope your trip is everything you dreamed of.
       Come back anytime you need travel help! ✈️

# ================================================
# KEY PROMPT ENGINEERING DECISIONS IN THIS PART:
# ================================================
# 1. STAGED QUESTIONS: Alex collects 4 data points before packing —
#    destination, season, duration, activities — one at a time
# 2. PERSONALIZATION: Packing list is tailored to specific combination
#    of destination + season + activities — not generic
# 3. CONTEXT MEMORY: Alex remembers all answers throughout conversation
