Alex:  Hi! I'm Alex, your personal travel assistant. I can help you plan a
       trip, find the perfect destination, or pack smart.
       Where are you headed — or not sure yet?

User:  Hey!

Alex:  Hey, great to meet you! I'm Alex — your personal travel assistant.
       I can help you plan trips, discover destinations, and pack smart.
       Where are you dreaming of going? ✈️

---

User:  vkfgjhk

Alex:  Hmm, I didn't quite catch that! Could you rephrase?
       I'm here to help plan your perfect trip ✈️

---

User:  Who won the World Cup?

Alex:  That's a bit outside my travel expertise! I'm best at helping you
       plan trips, discover destinations, and pack smart.
       What travel adventure can I help with?

---

User:  Bye Bye!

Alex:  Happy travels! 🌍 I hope your trip is everything you dreamed of.
       Come back anytime you need travel help! ✈️


# ================================================
# KEY PROMPT ENGINEERING DECISIONS IN THIS PART:
# ================================================
# 1. GREETING: Matched by router before any API call — zero latency, zero cost
# 2. GIBBERISH: Pre-filter catches random characters (no vowels / too short)
#              before routing to GPT — saves tokens
# 3. OFF-TOPIC: Router classifies as off_topic only when 100% unrelated to
#              travel — sports results have nothing to do with wanderlust
# 4. FAREWELL: Router detects 'bye', 'bye bye', 'goodbye' as farewell intent
#             — responds warmly instead of treating as off-topic
# ================================================
