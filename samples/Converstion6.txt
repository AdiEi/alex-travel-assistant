Alex:  Hi! I'm Alex, your personal travel assistant.
       Where are you headed — or not sure yet?
 
User:  How far will my budget go?
 
Alex:  I'd love to help! Could you share your total budget amount?
 
User:  what is exchange rate?
 
Alex:  I only have verified rates for major currencies.
       Please check XE.com for this currency.
       What's your total budget amount?
 
User:  I have $2500
 
Alex:  Your $2,500 is approximately ¥394,550 JPY.
       To help you see how far this budget can stretch,
       what's your intended destination?
 
User:  I want to travel to Israel
 
Alex:  Israel is such a vibrant choice!
       Your $2,500 is approximately 7,275 ILS (live rate).
       How many days are you planning to stay?
 
User:  I don't know
 
Alex:  No worries! Are you leaning towards a longer stay
       for exploration, or a shorter trip for a specific
       occasion? This will help us figure out a good duration.
 
--- [Connection error occurred here — Alex recovered gracefully] ---
 
User:  What should I pack?
 
Alex:  Before I help with packing, when are you planning
       to travel? (month or season)
 
       [Note: Alex remembered Israel from before the error]
 
User:  summer
 
Alex:  Got it! Summer in Israel. How many days are you
       traveling and what activities are planned?
 
User:  around 10 days
 
Alex:  What main activities are planned during your 10 days
       in Israel? Beach, historical sites, hiking, or city life?
 
User:  relax at the beach
 
Alex:  A beach getaway in Israel for 10 days in summer,
       here's your tailored packing list:
 
       Clothing:
       - Swimsuits (2-3)
       - Cover-ups or sarongs
       - Lightweight shorts, tank tops, sundresses
       - Light sweater for cooler evenings
 
       Beach essentials:
       - Lightweight beach towels
       - Sunglasses and wide-brimmed hat
       - Flip-flops or sandals
       - Reef-safe sunscreen
       - Beach bag
 
       Hydration:
       - Reusable water bottle, Israel summers are hot!
 
User:  No, Thanks!
 
Alex:  Happy travels! I hope your trip is everything
       you dreamed of. Come back anytime you need travel help!
